silly city is a webring for

  • nerds
  • sillies
  • queers

to join, submit a PR with a little note and your details in /ring/citizens.json (a username, and the address of your site). alternatively, email mayor [at] silly.city with a patch or with your relevant details.
then place the iframe on your site or use the appropriate links to make sure people can get to the other sites in the ring from yours :)

replace your-username in the below URLs with your username when adding into your site
